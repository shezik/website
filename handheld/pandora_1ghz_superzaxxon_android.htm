掌機 - Pandora (1GHz) - SuperZaxxon - 執行Android系統


安裝步驟：
1. http://notaz.gp2x.de/releases/pandora/gingerbread_r6.pnd(放到/pandora/menu資料夾)
2. http://goo.im/gapps/gapps-gb-20110828-signed.zip(解壓縮到/pandora/appdata/android_gingerbread資料夾)
3. 刪除/pandora/appdata/android_gingerbread/meta-inf資料夾
4. 將SD放到左邊的插槽，從System => Android Gingerbread進入Android系統
