掌機 - Pandora (Rebirth) - SuperZaxxon - SDL環境變數


參考資訊：
https://notaz.gp2x.de/cgi-bin/gitweb.cgi?p=sdl_omap.git;a=blob;f=README.OMAP

變數名稱說明
SDL_VIDEODRIVER=omapdss
SDL_OMAP_LAYER_SIZE=WxH (如：640x480)
=fullscreen (全屏顯示)
=scaled (等比拉伸)
=pixelperfect (整數倍拉伸)
SDL_OMAP_BORDER_CUT=left,right,top,bottom (如：0,0,16,0，隱藏Top的16個像素)
SDL_OMAP_VSYNC=1 (SDL_Flip()會等待VSYNC)
=0 (SDL_Flip()不會等待VSYNC)
SDL_OMAP_DEFAULT_MODE=WxH (取代SDL_SetVideoMode()的設定值)
SDL_OMAP_FORCE_DOUBLEBUF強制使用Double Buffer
SDL_OMAP_FORCE_DIRECTBUF直接更新Framebuffer
SDL_OMAP_NO_TS_TRANSLATE關閉Touchscreen映射
SDL_OMAP_TS_FORCE_TSLIB強制從tslib讀取Touchscreen數值
SDL_OMAP_NO_PANDORA_TV關閉TVout的Framebuffer更新

P.S. Filter會呼叫/usr/pandora/scripts/op_videofir.sh，default(預設值)、none(沒有濾波)
