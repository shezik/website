手機 - PINE64 PinePhone - postmarketOS - 安裝系統


參考資料：
https://wiki.pine64.org/index.php/PinePhone
https://wiki.pine64.org/wiki/PinePhone_Software_Releases

步驟如下：
1. 安裝Tow-Boot
2. 執行如下命令： 

$ cd
$ wget https://images.postmarketos.org/bpo/v22.12/pine64-pinephone/phosh/20230219-0413/20230219-0413-postmarketOS-v22.12-phosh-22.2-pine64-pinephone-installer.img.xz
$ xz -d 20230219-0413-postmarketOS-v22.12-phosh-22.2-pine64-pinephone-installer.img.xz
$ sudo dd if=20230219-0413-postmarketOS-v22.12-phosh-22.2-pine64-pinephone-installer.img of=/dev/sdX bs=1M
$ sync

完成
