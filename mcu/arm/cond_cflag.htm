ARM

Carry Flag

參考資訊：
1. carry-flag

For non-additions/subtractions that incorporate a shift operation, C is set to the last bit shifted out of the value by the shifter.

範例：

指令Carry Flag
ldr r0, =0x00000000
movs r1, r0, lsr#1		C=0
ldr r0, =0x00000001
movs r1, r0, lsr#1		C=1
ldr r0, =0x40000000
movs r1, r0, lsl#2		C=1
ldr r0, =0x00000000
orrs r1, r0, lsr#1		C=0
ldr r0, =0x00000001
orrs r1, r0, lsr#1		C=1
ldr r0, =0x40000000
orrs r1, r0, lsl#2		C=1
