BCM2835 >> QEMU

安裝系統

參考資訊：
1. using-qemu-to-emulate-a-raspberry-pi

步驟如下： 

$ cd
$ wget https://github.com/dhruvvyas90/qemu-rpi-kernel/raw/master/kernel-qemu-4.4.34-jessie
$ wget http://ftp.jaist.ac.jp/pub/raspberrypi/raspbian_full/images/raspbian_full-2020-02-14/2020-02-13-raspbian-buster-full.zip
$ unzip 2020-02-13-raspbian-buster-full.zip
$ qemu-img convert -f raw -O qcow2 2020-02-13-raspbian-buster-full.img 2020-02-13-raspbian-buster-full.qcow
$ qemu-img resize 2020-02-13-raspbian-buster-full.qcow +6G
$ sudo qemu-system-arm -kernel ./kernel-qemu-4.4.34-jessie -append "root=/dev/sda2 panic=1 rootfstype=ext4 rw" -hda 2020-02-13-raspbian-buster-full.qcow -cpu arm1176 -m 256 -M versatilepb -no-reboot -serial stdio


P.S. pi:raspberry
