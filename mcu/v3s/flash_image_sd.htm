Flash Image(SDCard)

步驟如下： 

$ cd
$ wget https://github.com/steward-fu/v3s_image/blob/master/brmin_dd.tar.bz2
$ tar xvf brmin_dd.tar.bz2
$ sudo dd if=brmin_dd/lichee_zero-brmin_alpha.dd of=/dev/sdx
$ sync
